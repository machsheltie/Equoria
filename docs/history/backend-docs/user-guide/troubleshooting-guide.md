### Account & Authentication Problems

#### **Cannot Log In**
**Symptoms**: Login fails, "Invalid credentials" error
**Solutions**:
1. **Check Credentials**: Verify username/email and password
2. **Password Reset**: Use "Forgot Password" link if needed
3. **Account Status**: Ensure account is active and verified
4. **Browser Issues**: Clear cache and cookies, try different browser
5. **Server Status**: Check if game servers are online

#### **Session Expires Frequently**
**Symptoms**: Logged out unexpectedly, need to re-login often
**Solutions**:
1. **Browser Settings**: Enable cookies and local storage
2. **Security Software**: Whitelist Equoria in antivirus/firewall
3. **Network Issues**: Check for stable internet connection
4. **Multiple Tabs**: Avoid opening multiple game tabs simultaneously

#### **Account Data Missing**
**Symptoms**: Horses, progress, or money disappeared
**Solutions**:
1. **Server Sync**: Wait 5-10 minutes for data synchronization
2. **Browser Refresh**: Hard refresh (Ctrl+F5) the page
3. **Different Device**: Check if data appears on other devices
4. **Contact Support**: Submit ticket with account details

### Horse Management Issues

#### **Cannot Train Horse**
**Symptoms**: Training button disabled, error messages
**Common Causes & Solutions**:

**Age Restriction**:
- **Problem**: Horse under 3 years old
- **Solution**: Wait until horse reaches 3 years of age

**Training Cooldown**:
- **Problem**: Already trained this week (7-day cooldown)
- **Solution**: Wait for cooldown to expire, check training status

**Health Issues**:
- **Problem**: Horse health is Poor or Injured
- **Solution**: Improve horse health through rest and care

**Missing Trait Requirements**:
- **Problem**: Gaited discipline requires Gaited trait
- **Solution**: Only train Gaited horses in Gaited discipline

#### **Horse Won't Compete**
**Symptoms**: Cannot enter competitions, eligibility errors
**Common Causes & Solutions**:

**Age Restrictions**:
- **Problem**: Horse too young (under 3) or too old (over 21)
- **Solution**: Only compete horses aged 3-21 years

**Health Requirements**:
- **Problem**: Horse health below competition standards
- **Solution**: Improve health to Good or Excellent status

**Skill Level Too Low**:
- **Problem**: Horse doesn't meet minimum discipline requirements
- **Solution**: Train horse in relevant discipline first

**Show Entry Issues**:
- **Problem**: Show is full or entry period closed
- **Solution**: Enter earlier or find alternative competitions

#### **Breeding Problems**
**Symptoms**: Cannot breed horses, breeding fails
**Common Causes & Solutions**:

**Age Requirements**:
- **Problem**: Mare or stallion under 3 years old
- **Solution**: Wait until both horses reach breeding age

**Breeding Cooldown**:
- **Problem**: Horse recently bred (cooldown active)
- **Solution**: Wait for breeding cooldown to expire

**Health Issues**:
- **Problem**: Poor health affects breeding success
- **Solution**: Ensure both horses have Good+ health

**Genetic Compatibility**:
- **Problem**: Horses too closely related
- **Solution**: Choose unrelated breeding partners

### Groom Management Issues

#### **Cannot Hire Groom**
**Symptoms**: Hiring fails, insufficient funds error
**Solutions**:
1. **Check Funds**: Ensure sufficient money for hiring costs
2. **Stable Capacity**: Verify you haven't reached groom limit
3. **Skill Availability**: Some skill levels may be temporarily unavailable
4. **Refresh Market**: Wait for new grooms to become available

#### **Groom Interactions Failing**
**Symptoms**: Cannot assign tasks, interaction errors
**Common Causes & Solutions**:

**Daily Limits**:
- **Problem**: Already interacted with horse today
- **Solution**: Wait until next day (24-hour cooldown)

**Age Restrictions**:
- **Problem**: Wrong task type for horse age
- **Solution**: Use age-appropriate tasks (enrichment for foals, grooming for adults)

**Horse Ownership**:
- **Problem**: Trying to groom horse you don't own
- **Solution**: Only assign grooms to your own horses

**Groom Availability**:
- **Problem**: Groom already assigned or unavailable
- **Solution**: Check groom schedule and availability

#### **Groom Retirement Issues**
**Symptoms**: Unexpected retirement, cannot create legacy
**Solutions**:
1. **Career Length**: Grooms retire after 104 weeks (2 years)
2. **Early Retirement**: Level 10+ grooms can retire early
3. **Legacy Eligibility**: Only level 7+ grooms can create prot√©g√©s
4. **Replacement Planning**: Hire new grooms before retirements

### Competition & Performance Issues

#### **Poor Competition Results**
**Symptoms**: Horse consistently places poorly despite good stats
**Analysis & Solutions**:

**Competition Level Mismatch**:
- **Problem**: Entering competitions too advanced for horse level
- **Solution**: Start with appropriate level competitions, build gradually

**Discipline Mismatch**:
- **Problem**: Horse stats don't match discipline requirements
- **Solution**: Choose disciplines that match horse's strengths

**Training Deficiency**:
- **Problem**: Low discipline scores affect performance
- **Solution**: Focus training on competition disciplines

**Trait Conflicts**:
- **Problem**: Negative traits affecting performance
- **Solution**: Work on trait development through better care

**Age Factors**:
- **Problem**: Horse past peak performance age
- **Solution**: Adjust expectations for older horses

#### **Competition Entry Problems**
**Symptoms**: Cannot enter shows, eligibility issues
**Solutions**:
1. **Check Requirements**: Verify age, health, and skill minimums
2. **Entry Timing**: Enter before deadline, shows fill up
3. **Financial Requirements**: Ensure sufficient funds for entry fees
4. **Show Availability**: Some shows may be full or cancelled

### Technical Issues

#### **Game Loading Problems**
**Symptoms**: Slow loading, timeouts, blank screens
**Solutions**:
1. **Internet Connection**: Check network stability and speed
2. **Browser Cache**: Clear browser cache and cookies
3. **Browser Compatibility**: Use supported browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. **Ad Blockers**: Disable ad blockers that might interfere
5. **JavaScript**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled
6. **Server Status**: Check game status page for outages

#### **Performance Issues**
**Symptoms**: Slow response, lag, freezing
**Solutions**:
1. **Close Other Tabs**: Reduce browser memory usage
2. **Restart Browser**: Fresh start can resolve memory issues
3. **Update Browser**: Use latest browser version
4. **System Resources**: Close unnecessary programs
5. **Graphics Settings**: Reduce visual effects if available

#### **Data Synchronization Issues**
**Symptoms**: Changes not saving, data inconsistencies
**Solutions**:
1. **Wait for Sync**: Allow 30 seconds for automatic saving
2. **Manual Refresh**: Refresh page to force data sync
3. **Network Check**: Ensure stable internet connection
4. **Multiple Devices**: Avoid playing on multiple devices simultaneously
5. **Browser Storage**: Clear local storage if data conflicts occur

### Contact Support

#### **When to Contact Support**
- Account access issues that persist after troubleshooting
- Missing data or progress that doesn't restore
- Payment or billing problems
- Suspected bugs or technical issues
- Harassment or inappropriate behavior reports

#### **How to Contact Support**
1. **Support Ticket System**: Most comprehensive option
2. **In-Game Help**: Quick access to common solutions
3. **Email Support**: For detailed issues requiring documentation
4. **Community Moderators**: For urgent issues in forums/Discord

#### **Information to Include**
- **Account Details**: Username, email (never include password)
- **Issue Description**: Clear explanation of the problem
- **Steps Taken**: What troubleshooting you've already tried
- **Screenshots**: Visual evidence of issues when helpful
- **Browser/Device**: Technical specifications if relevant
- **Timing**: When the issue started occurring

### Bug Reporting

#### **How to Report Bugs**
1. **Reproduce the Issue**: Confirm it happens consistently
2. **Document Steps**: Record exact steps to trigger the bug
3. **Gather Evidence**: Screenshots or video if possible
4. **Check Known Issues**: Review existing bug reports
5. **Submit Report**: Use official bug reporting channels

#### **Good Bug Reports Include**
- **Clear Title**: Concise description of the issue
- **Reproduction Steps**: Numbered list of actions
- **Expected Behavior**: What should happen
- **Actual Behavior**: What actually happens
- **Environment**: Browser, device, operating system
- **Frequency**: How often it occurs

- **Cause**: Not enough money for the action
- **Solution**: Earn more money through competitions or sales

- **Cause**: Horse trained within last 7 days
- **Solution**: Wait for cooldown to expire

#### **"Horse Too Young"**
- **Cause**: Horse under minimum age for activity
- **Solution**: Wait for horse to reach required age

#### **"Invalid Session"**
- **Cause**: Login session expired
- **Solution**: Log out and log back in

#### **"Server Error"**
- **Cause**: Temporary server issue
