## üê¥ Horse Management

### Understanding Horse Attributes

#### **Core Statistics (0-100)**
- **Speed** - How fast your horse can run
- **Stamina** - Endurance for longer competitions
- **Agility** - Quick movements and responsiveness
- **Balance** - Stability and coordination
- **Precision** - Accuracy in movements
- **Intelligence** - Learning ability and problem-solving
- **Boldness** - Confidence in challenging situations
- **Flexibility** - Physical adaptability
- **Obedience** - Willingness to follow commands
- **Focus** - Concentration and attention span

#### **Health & Temperament**
- **Health Status** - Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor, Injured
- **Temperament** - Calm, Confident, Energetic, Nervous, Aggressive
- **Age** - Affects performance and breeding eligibility
- **Bonding Level** - Relationship strength with you

### Horse Development

#### **Age Stages**
- **Foal (0-1 years)** - Critical development period
- **Yearling (1-2 years)** - Early training begins
- **Young Horse (2-3 years)** - Basic training eligible
- **Adult (3-21 years)** - Full competition and breeding
- **Retired (21+ years)** - No longer competes

#### **Training System**
- **Age Requirement** - Horses must be 3+ years old
- **Weekly Cooldown** - One training session per week total
- **Discipline Focus** - Choose from 6 training disciplines:
  - Racing, Dressage, Show Jumping, Cross Country, Western, Gaited
- **Skill Improvement** - +5 discipline score per session
- **XP Rewards** - Gain experience points for training
- **Stat Bonuses** - 15% chance for random stat increases

#### **Trait Discovery**

- **Care Quality** - Better care increases positive trait chances
- **Milestone Events** - Critical development periods
- **Groom Interactions** - Professional care influences traits

## üèÜ Competition System

### Competition Disciplines (24 Available)
Equoria features the most comprehensive competition system with 24 unique disciplines:

#### **Racing Disciplines**
- **Racing** - Flat track speed competitions
- **Steeplechase** - Racing with jumps and obstacles
- **Harness Racing** - Trotting and pacing with sulky

#### **English Disciplines**
- **Dressage** - Precision and elegance in movement
- **Show Jumping** - Jumping courses with time and accuracy
- **Cross Country** - Endurance jumping across terrain
- **Eventing** - Three-phase competition (dressage, cross-country, show jumping)
- **Hunter** - Style and form over fences

#### **Western Disciplines**
- **Western Pleasure** - Smooth, comfortable gaits
- **Reining** - Precision patterns with spins and stops
- **Cutting** - Separating cattle from herd
- **Barrel Racing** - Speed around barrel pattern
- **Roping** - Cattle roping competitions
- **Team Penning** - Teamwork to sort cattle
- **Rodeo** - Various rodeo events

#### **Specialized Disciplines**
- **Gaited** - Special gaited horse competitions (requires Gaited trait)
- **Saddleseat** - High-stepping show ring performance
- **Endurance** - Long-distance competitions
- **Vaulting** - Gymnastics on horseback
- **Polo** - Team sport on horseback
- **Combined Driving** - Carriage driving competitions
- **Fine Harness** - Elegant harness horse showing
- **Gymkhana** - Timed games and patterns
- **Obedience Training** - Training and behavior competitions

### Competition Mechanics

#### **Entry Requirements**
- **Age** - Horses must be 3-21 years old
- **Health** - Good health required
- **Skill Level** - Minimum discipline scores
- **Special Requirements** - Some disciplines need specific traits

#### **Scoring System**
- **Multi-Factor Calculation** - Stats, traits, age, training, and performance variance
- **Hidden Scores** - You see placement, not raw scores
- **Realistic Variance** - Even top horses don't always win
- **Age Performance Curves** - Peak performance at ages 6-8

#### **Rewards**
- **Prize Money** - 1st: 50%, 2nd: 30%, 3rd: 20% of purse
- **User XP** - 1st: 20 XP, 2nd: 15 XP, 3rd: 10 XP
- **Horse XP** - 1st: 30 XP, 2nd: 27 XP, 3rd: 25 XP
- **Stat Bonuses** - Random stat increases for top 3 finishers
- **Reputation** - Build your stable's reputation
