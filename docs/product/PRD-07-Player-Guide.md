## Overview

This document serves as the comprehensive player guide for Equoria, covering all game mechanics, features, and strategic approaches for building a successful horse breeding empire.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating Your Account

1. **Register** with a unique username and email
2. **Choose your stable name** - this represents your breeding operation
3. **Receive starter funds** - $1,000 to begin your journey
4. **Start with basic facilities** - expand as you grow

### 1.2 Your First Horse

- **Purchase from marketplace** or **adopt a starter horse**
- **Choose your breed carefully** - each has unique characteristics
- **Name your horse** - make it memorable!
- **Review stats and traits** - these determine performance potential

```
Performance Curve:
```

## 3. Training System

### 3.1 Training Requirements

- **Age Requirement:** Horses must be 3+ years old
- **Health Requirement:** Good or Excellent health status
- **Cooldown:** One training session per week total (global)

### 4.2 Competition Scoring System

#### Horse Level Calculation
```
Horse Level = (Base Stats × 0.4) + (Training Scores × 0.3) + (Trait Bonuses × 0.2) + (Age Factor × 0.1)
```

#### Level Scaling
| Level Range | Points Required |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Levels 1-10 | Every 50 points (0-499 total) |
| Levels 11-20 | Every 100 points (500-1499 total) |
| Levels 21+ | Every 150 points (1500+ total) |

### 4.3 Competition Rewards

## 5. Breeding & Genetics

### 5.1 Breeding Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum Age** | 3 years |
| **Maximum Age** | 21 years (optimal: 5-15) |
| **Health Status** | Good or Excellent |
| **Breeding Cooldown** | 30 days (mare), 14 days (stallion) |
| **Gestation Period** | Realistic timelines |

### 5.2 Genetic Inheritance

#### Stat Inheritance
```
Foal Stat = (Sire Stat + Dam Stat) / 2 ± 10% variance
```

#### Trait Inheritance
- **Dominant Traits:** Higher inheritance rates (60-80%)
- **Recessive Traits:** Lower inheritance rates (20-40%)
- **Hidden Traits:** Discovered through conditions, not inherited directly
- **Epigenetic Traits:** Influenced by environment and care

### 5.3 Epigenetic Trait System

#### Trait Categories
| Category | Examples | Discovery Method |
|----------|----------|------------------|
| **Positive** | BRAVE, INTELLIGENT, ATHLETIC | High-quality care, milestones |
| **Negative** | ANXIOUS, STUBBORN, LAZY | Poor care, trauma |
| **Hidden** | LEGENDARY, MYTHIC | Special conditions |

#### Trait Discovery Factors
- **Environmental Triggers:** Weather, terrain, social exposure
- **Care Quality:** Groom interactions, enrichment activities
- **Milestone Events:** First competition, training achievements
- **Bonding Level:** Higher bonding reveals more traits

### 5.4 Foal Development (Critical First 6 Days)

| Day | Focus Area | Activities |
|-----|------------|------------|
| 1 | Imprinting | Initial bonding, human contact |
| 2 | Trust Building | Gentle handling, voice exposure |
| 3 | Desensitization | New objects, sounds |
| 4 | Social Development | Other horses, herd dynamics |
| 5 | Physical Development | Movement, coordination |
| 6 | Environmental Exposure | Different areas, surfaces |

## 8. Strategic Tips

### 8.1 Building a Successful Stable

1. **Start Small:** Focus on quality over quantity (3-5 horses)
2. **Specialize Early:** Choose 2-3 disciplines to master
3. **Invest in Grooms:** Professional care makes significant difference
4. **Plan Breeding:** Think 3-4 generations ahead
5. **Track Performance:** Use data to make informed decisions

### 8.2 Competition Strategy

1. **Know Your Horse:** Understand strengths and weaknesses
2. **Choose Appropriate Competitions:** Match horse to discipline
3. **Prepare Thoroughly:** Training and care before competing
4. **Build Gradually:** Start with easier competitions
5. **Consistency Over Wins:** Regular placings beat occasional wins

### 8.3 Breeding Excellence

1. **Study Genetics:** Understand trait inheritance
2. **Plan Matings:** Choose complementary parents
3. **Invest in Foal Care:** Critical early development
4. **Track Lineages:** Maintain detailed breeding records
5. **Patience Pays:** Genetic improvement takes generations

### 8.4 The 1000-Point Horse

To reach the highest competitive levels:
1. **Genetic Foundation:** Start with 700+ point parents
2. **Optimal Training:** Maximize discipline scores
3. **Trait Optimization:** Stack 4-5 beneficial traits
4. **Age Management:** Compete during peak years (6-8)
5. **Care Excellence:** Use professional groom services
